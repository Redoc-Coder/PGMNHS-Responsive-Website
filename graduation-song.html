PEDRO GUEVARA MEMORIAL
NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL

Graduation Song

Laguna Beloved

O Laguna, Laguna beloved,

Land of beauty of sunrise and songs,

Thou apart from the bright skies removed,

Still our poor hearts for thee everlong.


With the blue skies the hills and the mountains,

For the dear land that gave us our birth,

How we long for the hills and the mountains,

For the dearest and fairest on earth (3x)

Farewell Song

High school days are happy days together,

Still we know that we must say good bye,

All the joys that we have known is over,

And the memories of you will never die.


Farewell dear friends,

Farewell teachers dear our days

are coming to an end,

And we will hope for better days to come

When friends shall meet with friends.
